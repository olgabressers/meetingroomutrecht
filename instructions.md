# meetingroomutrecht.nl — Project Instructions

## Overview

This project is a website for renting out a private living room as a unique meeting and event location in Utrecht, available at **meetingroomutrecht.nl**.

## The Space

- **Living room** — the main bookable space, suitable for intimate group sessions
- **Private office room** — separate room for focused work or 1-on-1 sessions
- **Big sunny garden** — outdoor space available as part of the booking

## Target Use Cases

The location is suitable for a variety of bookings:

- **Business meetings** — small team meetings, strategy sessions, offsites
- **Coaching sessions** — 1-on-1 or small group coaching
- **Yoga & movement** — yoga classes, breathwork, somatic sessions
- **Energetic / healing work** — energy healing, sound baths, spiritual sessions
- **Creative sessions** — workshops, brainstorming, creative retreats

## Website Goals

- Allow visitors to learn about the space and what it offers
- Enable people to book the space (or inquire about availability)
- Communicate the unique, personal character of the location
- Appeal to both professional and wellness/creative audiences
